Father's Day

For Dad

The older I get, the more I realize how deeply I love all of you, how important you are to me, and how much I cherish the time we get to spend together.

Dad, you’ve inspired me to become a better man in so many ways. You’ve known when to be tough with me, when to guide me, and when to be gentle and understanding. I’m grateful for the thought, care, and love you put into our family every day.

Your faith journey has also been incredibly inspiring, especially through these worrying times. Thank you for everything you do and for the man you continue to be.

I’m proud of you, Dad. I love you.
